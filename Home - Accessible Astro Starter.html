Stephen Christopher

Anthropologist of Religion

I am an anthropologist of religion, specializing in Himalayan tribes, the Tibetan diaspora and global Buddhism. My research focuses on how minority religions engage with the state. I have taught over 1,000 students in nine universities and co-lead a major digital humanities project on new religiosity.
